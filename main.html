Movie Summary

Inception (2010) is a science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The story follows Dom Cobb, a skilled "extractor" who is tasked with performing the impossible: "inception," planting an idea in someone's mind through their dreams. As Cobb and his team navigate the layers of the dream world, they must confront their own demons and face the blurred line between dreams and reality. The film is known for its mind-bending plot and stunning visual effects.

Actors

Leonardo DiCaprio plays the lead role of Dom Cobb, the expert thief who specializes in stealing secrets from deep within the subconscious.

Joseph Gordon-Levitt portrays Arthur, Cobb's trusted partner and dream architect.

Ellen Page (now known as Elliot Page) plays Ariadne, a young architect who helps design the complex dreamscapes.
