summary

Inception is a science fiction action film directed by Christopher Nolan. The story follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who specializes in the art of "extraction" – stealing secrets from within a person's subconscious while they dream. Cobb is offered a chance to have his criminal record erased if he can successfully perform "inception," planting an idea in someone's mind through their dreams. The movie explores complex themes like dreams within dreams, reality, and human consciousness.
